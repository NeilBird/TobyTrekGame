## About

This game is a tribute to Toby the cat, featuring a cheerful bird character navigating through green pipes. Test your reflexes and try to beat your high score!

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Click the "Start Game" button
3. Click anywhere on the canvas or press SPACE to make the bird flap
4. Avoid the obstacles (pipes)
5. Try to get the highest score possible!
